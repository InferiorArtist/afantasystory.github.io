Join us for the adventure in the land of Mar'Ee!

A Fantasy Story will follow Bale, who is an unruly scoundrel who barely makes ends meat through odd jobs and small security work. He is stuck in the past when bloodtech is the prevailing technological advancement of this time and wage disparity becomes larger and larger. He is hired by a shifty, smaller than average fellow to guard a mysterious box marked with religious engravings and runes. Unbeknownst to Bale, he will be involved in something greater than he will ever imagine.
